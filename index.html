About Us

Established in 2014, ITS Ocean & Oil Ltd stands as a fully integrated upstream and downstream oil and gas company based in Nigeria, committed to excellence in petroleum product distribution.

Our services cover the entire value chain, including storage, haulage, and retailing of essential fuels like Petrol (PMS), Diesel (AGO), and Kerosene (DPK).
